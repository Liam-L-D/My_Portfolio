Project Overview

A milestone identity for a community celebration.

The Chinese Community Center commissioned a visual communication system for its 50th Anniversary celebration. The project included an anniversary logo, booklet layout, invitation design, and event display materials that worked together as one consistent identity for the milestone event.

Role

Visual communication design, anniversary logo design, booklet layout, invitation design, banner design, and print material preparation.

Tools

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign, and print-ready layout workflow.

Anniversary Logo Booklet Design Invitation Design Roll-Up Banner Event Branding
Challenge & Solution

Building a consistent event identity across multiple formats.

The challenge was to create a recognizable anniversary identity that could work across invitations, booklets, banners, and promotional materials. A unified visual language, typography system, and color direction ensured consistency across every touchpoint of the celebration.

Anniversary Identity

Designed a 50th-year logo that communicates celebration, community, cultural heritage, and a sense of organizational history.

Print Layout System

Created booklet and invitation layouts with clear content hierarchy, image placement, spacing, and print-friendly structure.

Event Display

Extended the anniversary identity to large-format materials so the event could feel consistent across physical spaces.

Selected Outputs

Anniversary campaign deliverables.

A focused selection of print and event materials designed to create a consistent visual identity for the Chinese Community Center's 50th Anniversary celebration.
