Project Overview

Turning city identity into a chocolate packaging system.

Flavor Journey is a chocolate packaging concept inspired by New York, Chicago, Miami, and Los Angeles. Each city is paired with a regional flavor and translated into its own visual identity.

Beginning with logo development and package structure, the project evolved into a complete packaging system featuring city-inspired graphics, flavor-based branding, and final product mockups.

Scope

Branding, packaging design, logo development, dieline layout, illustration, and product visualization.

Deliverables

Design brief, package structure, city flavor logo system, final packaging artwork, and chocolate product mockups.

Designing a system instead of a single package.

This project strengthened my understanding of packaging as a complete design system. By connecting branding, structure, illustration, and regional storytelling, I developed a collection that balances consistency with variation across multiple product identities.
